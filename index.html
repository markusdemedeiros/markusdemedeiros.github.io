About Me

I'm Markus, a PhD student at NYU working with Joseph Tassarotti. Lately, I have been working on the Clutch logic.

I am broadly interested on formal methods, at present I am interested in methods for proving privacy and security properties of systems at a realistic scale.

Previously, I worked on about automatically translating type-correctness information from the Rust compiler into an explicit memory safety proof, as part of the Prusti project with Alex Summers at UBC.

I have also worked on SampCert, during an internship at AWS in the summer of 2024.
